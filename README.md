# KnowledgeCivic

KnowledgeCivic is an open civic learning commons: a place where people learn how public life works, examine evidence together, and help maintain knowledge that others can use.

## Why this exists

Education is not only preparation for work. It helps people understand rights, institutions, evidence, shared problems, and the choices available to a community. KnowledgeCivic treats lifelong learning as a civic practice rather than a credential reserved for specialists.

## Our commitments

- Open access and reusable materials.
- Evidence with visible sources and dates.
- Plain language without flattening complexity.
- Multiple perspectives without pretending every claim has equal support.
- Accessibility, translation, and local adaptation.
- Non-partisanship, transparency, and correction.
